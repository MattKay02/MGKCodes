# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in the MGKCodes website, please
report it responsibly by emailing:

**hello@mgkcodes.com**

Please include:

- A clear description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce
- The deployed URL or commit hash where you observed the issue
- Any relevant logs, screenshots, or proof-of-concept code

We aim to acknowledge reports within 72 hours and provide an initial
assessment within 7 days. Please do not publicly disclose the vulnerability
until we have had a reasonable opportunity to investigate and respond.

## Scope

In scope:

- The MGKCodes studio website (mgkcodes.com) and its hosting infrastructure

Out of scope:

- Individual MGKCodes products (e.g. Liftio, getliftio.com) — these have
  their own security policies in their respective repositories
- Third-party services we integrate with — please report directly to the
  relevant vendor

## Acknowledgements

We appreciate responsible disclosure. With your permission, we will
acknowledge your contribution publicly once the issue is resolved.
